Endangered Ocean Animals

Vaquita Porpoise

CRITICALLY ENDANGERED

With fewer than 10 individuals remaining, the vaquita is the world's most endangered marine mammal.

Blue Whale

ENDANGERED

The largest animal on Earth, blue whales were hunted nearly to extinction and now face threats from ship strikes and ocean noise.

Sea Otter

ENDANGERED

Once hunted for their fur, sea otters play a crucial role in maintaining kelp forest ecosystems by controlling sea urchin populations.

North Atlantic Right Whale

CRITICALLY ENDANGERED

With only about 350 remaining, these whales are threatened by ship strikes and entanglement in fishing gear.

Green Sea Turtle

ENDANGERED

Named for the green color of their fat, these turtles face threats from habitat loss, bycatch, and pollution.
