Threats & Sustainable Solutions

Major Threats to Marine Life

Plastic Pollution

Over 8 million tons of plastic enter the ocean yearly, harming marine life.

Overfishing

90% of global fish stocks are overexploited, leading to ecosystem collapse.

Coral Bleaching

corals lose their vibrant colors and turn white due to the expulsion of symbiotic algae called zooxanthellae.

Climate Change

Rising ocean temperatures kill coral reefs, affecting 25% of marine species.

Oil Spill

Oil can suffocate or poison marine animals, damage their habitat, and disrupt their feeding and breeding patterns.

Noise Pollution

What Can We Do?

Reduce Plastic Use

Switch to reusable bags, bottles, and straws.

Support Sustainable Seafood

Check labels for MSC-certified fish.

Join Beach Cleanups

Volunteer to remove trash from coastlines.

Renewable Energy

shift towards the energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a faster rate than they are consumed.
